Training systems

Discover impactful exercise plans

Full body workout

A full body workout is a comprehensive exercise routine that targets multiple muscle groups throughout the entire body in a single session. It is designed to provide a balanced workout, engaging various muscle groups jointly or sequentially.Full body workouts typically include exercises that involve the upper body, lower body, and core muscles, providing a well-rounded approach to strength training and cardiovascular fitness

Upper and lower body workout

An upper and lower body workout is a comprehensive exercise routine that divides the training session into two main segments, targeting the upper body and lower body separately. The upper body segment includes exercises for the muscles in the chest, back, shoulders, arms, and core, while the lower body segment focuses on the muscles in the legs, glutes, and lower back.

Push and pull workout

A push and pull workout is a training routine that focuses on exercises categorized into two main movements: pushing and pulling. The push exercises primarily target the muscles involved in pushing movements, such as chest, shoulders, and triceps, while the pull exercises target muscles involved in pulling movements, such as back, biceps, and rear shoulders.
